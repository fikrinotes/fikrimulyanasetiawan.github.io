About Intergalactic War

Intergalactic war is a game with the concept of war between humans and extraterrestrials. This game tells the fate of humans if extraterrestrials really exist and we fight against them. This game was created by Fikri Mulyana Setiawan, a Programmer and Science Enthusiast. This game is inspired by Elon Musk's game called "blastar".

The Game's Rules

The UFO will move randomly from left to right to shoot you. your job is to dodge fire from UFOs and shoot UFOs back with unlimited bullets you have. Every time you shoot a UFO, your score will increase by 1 point. every time your score reaches multiples of 5 (5,10,15,20,..), the movement of the bullets from the UFO will be faster and increase the possibility for you to lose. Your life will be reduced by 25% every time you are shot by a UFO. However, if you get too close to the UFO, you will immediately lose.
